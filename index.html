Our Story

Authentic Bengali Heritage

Little Bangladesh brings you the rich culinary traditions of Bengal, passed down through generations. Our chefs use time-honored recipes and the finest spices to create dishes that transport you to the heart of Bangladesh.

From aromatic biryanis to delicate fish curries, every dish tells a story of our heritage and passion for authentic Bengali cuisine.

Founded in 2008 by Chef Rahman, who brought his grandmother's recipes from Dhaka to share the authentic taste of Bangladesh with food lovers around the world.
